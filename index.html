Vegetarian Food

Vegetarian food consists of plant-based ingredients, excluding meat, poultry, and seafood. It includes a variety of fresh vegetables, fruits, grains, legumes, nuts, and dairy products (in some vegetarian diets). Types of Vegetarian Diets Lacto-Vegetarian – Includes dairy products but no eggs or meat. Ovo-Vegetarian – Includes eggs but no dairy or meat. Lacto-Ovo Vegetarian – Includes both dairy and eggs but no meat. Vegan – Excludes all animal products, including dairy, eggs, and honey.
